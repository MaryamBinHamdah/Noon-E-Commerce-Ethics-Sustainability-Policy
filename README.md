# 🛒 Noon E-Commerce - Ethics and Sustainability Policy

This repository contains the comprehensive research and policy framework for "Noon E-Commerce," focusing on responsible data management and sustainable operations. This project was developed as the final requirement for the "Ethics and Sustainability for Computing (CIS 2013)" course.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The goal of this project was to design a robust Ethics and Sustainability Policy for Noon, the Middle East's leading e-commerce platform. We analyzed the ethical challenges of the digital economy, specifically focusing on data privacy, AI governance, and environmental responsibility to ensure alignment with the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).

### Key Objectives:
- **Ethical Analysis:** Identified core issues in data collection, algorithmic fairness, and third-party accountability.
- **Policy Development:** Proposed a framework covering data governance, operational sustainability, and supply chain ethics.
- **Legal Alignment:** Ensured the policy meets UAE PDPL requirements and integrates global GDPR best practices.
- **Stakeholder Mapping:** Defined the ethical roles of internal teams (developers, management) and external partners.

---

## 🛠️ Key Focus Areas

- **Data Governance & AI Ethics:** Rules for fair, transparent, and bias-free AI systems (dynamic pricing & recommendations).
- **Sustainability & Green IT:** Strategies for energy-efficient data centers, sustainable packaging, and electric delivery fleets.
- **Forensic Readiness:** Establishing digital trails and computer forensics protocols for secure and accountable data handling.

---

## 🎨 Research & Analysis Process

Our team followed a structured analytical approach to develop this policy:

1.  **Company Deep-Dive:** Analyzed Noon's data-driven ecosystem, including Noon Food, Noon Minutes, and Noon Pay.
2.  **Stakeholder Analysis:** Evaluated the ethical responsibilities of everyone from data scientists to delivery partners.
3.  **Regulatory Review:** Studied the UAE PDPL and Central Bank guidelines to ensure full legal compliance.
4.  **Sustainability Pillars:** Developed a three-pillar strategy covering Social Responsibility, IT Governance, and Sustainable Procurement.

---

## 💡 Roles & Key Learnings

This project provided deep insights into the intersection of technology and ethics. My main responsibilities included:
*   **Ethical Issues Identification:** Analyzed Noon’s data management lifecycle to identify risks like surveillance and the "black box" AI problem.
*   **Legal Framework Alignment:** Researched and ensured the policy’s alignment with the UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL).
*   **Sustainability Policy:** Developed the "Green IT" and sustainable procurement strategies to reduce environmental impact.
*   **Computer Forensics:** Defined the protocols for forensic readiness and secure digital trails to maintain platform accountability.

This project enhanced my understanding of how to build trust and responsibility into large-scale digital systems while balancing innovation with ethical and environmental standards.
